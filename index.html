About

After Britain’s exit from the European Union was announced, the Euro fluctuated drastically and has drawn caution to it’s residents and potential residents. Surprisingly, the EU keeps amazingly complete data on all of it’s countries and is free for the public to use. However, the immense amount of data deters users away from making any choices. Our vision is create a platform that allows EU residents to easily and visually understand and compare data on European countries, and make an informed decision regarding which country will be most suitable to call home.

Data Source

Eurostat-Your key to European statistics.

Years 2006 through 2015

Goal

Use Eurostat publicly available data to provide EU current or prospective residents with a self-exploratory tool, empowering them to compare countries and determine which would be the best for them to live in.
